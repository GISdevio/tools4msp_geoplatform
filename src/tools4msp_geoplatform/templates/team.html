Meet the Team

The design, development and implementation of the Tools4MSP modelling framework together with the maintenance and update of the Tools4MSP Geoplatform is coordinated by the Tools4MSP Team. The Team is part of the CNR-ISMAR ( Italian National Research Council - Institute of Marine Sciences ), Office of Venice. Currently, our research activities focus on the analysis of cumulative effects from human activities, trade-off-synergies analysis of multiple sea uses, ecosystem-based approach, integration of ecosystem services into MSP and development of open source tools to support MSP and ICZM.
